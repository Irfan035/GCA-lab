About Us

GCA

We are the top-ranked testing facility in the state of Missouri and strive to provide unparalleled, advanced, state-of-the-art, and state-compliant laboratory testing to deliver consistent, accurate results with a fast turnaround time. We provide rapid analysis and reporting of Cannabinoids (Potency), Terpenes, Moisture, Residual Solvents, Pesticides, Metals, Microbials, Mycotoxins, and more! Experience matters, and professionals at GCA Labs have decades of research, analytical, forensic, and clinical experience that will be used to serve your needs.

Our Mission

At GCA Labs, we believe in delivering world-class laboratory testing services and providing excellent customer support. By participating in our R&D services, we can help you troubleshoot problems and improve your processes with our rapid testing services and experienced team of scientists.
